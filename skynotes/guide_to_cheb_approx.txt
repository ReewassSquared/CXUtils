chebyshev approx.
convert x -> u: (2*x - a - b)/(b - a)
x = [a, b]

f(x) = sum(ck*Tk(u))

ck = (1/n)*sum(A(k)*Tk(uk)*f(xk))

where 
uk = cos((2*k - 1)*pi/(2*n))

u = 2x - 1
x = u+1/2

useful identities:
atan(-x) = -atan(x)
restricts [-inf, inf] to [0, inf]
atan(x) = - atan(1/x) - pi/2
restricts further to [0, 1]

choose N=16
